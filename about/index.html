About Drei Cafe

This is Drei Cafe, a newsletter / blog that's about 'literature' (a.k.a. good books, often by dead people) and fun life things like nicotine addiction and looking into stranger's windows. Literature is joyful and life-affirming and fun, I love it and want to share it with you, I don't care if the only book you read this year is The Barefoot Investor. I'm Drei, a Russian-Australian computer programmer and writer living in Melbourne, hi!

Thanks to the nation's favourite medical drama All Saints for the images.
